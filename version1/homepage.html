Welcome to Greek Connect!

Our goal is to create an all-in-one platform for memebers of Greek Life to expand and enhance their alumni network. Through Greek Connect undergraduates can send out announcements, flyers, and event invites to their alumni database all in one place.

Greek Connect will bring together vast alumni networks for members of greek life to further advance their professional development and career outlet.
